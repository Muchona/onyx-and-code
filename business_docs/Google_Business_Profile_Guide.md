# Google Business Profile - Registration Guide

**CRITICAL STEP:** This is the #1 way to appear in the "Map Pack" (the map list at the top of Google Search). Code helps, but this registration is mandatory.

## 1. Start the Process
1.  Go to [Google Business Profile](https://www.google.com/business/).
2.  Click **Manage now** using your business Google account (e.g., `muchona.onyx@gmail.com`).

## 2. Enter Business Name
1.  Type: **Onyx & Code**
2.  Category: Select **Web Designer** (Primary) and add **Software Company** / **Internet Marketing Service** as secondary categories later.

## 3. Location Settings
1.  **Do you want to add a location customers can visit?** 
    *   **YES:** If you have an office people can walk into.
    *   **NO:** If you work from home or remotely (Recommended if you don't have a public office).
2.  **Where do you serve your customers?**
    *   Add: **Monaghan**, **Dublin**, **Ireland**, **United Kingdom**.

## 4. Contact Details
1.  Add your phone number: **+353 89 445 9967** (Must match the website).
2.  Add your website: **https://onyxandcode.com**

## 5. Verification
*   Google will send a **Postcard** to your mailing address with a code.
*   **This can take 5-14 days.**
*   Once verified, your profile will go live.

## 6. Optimization (After Verification)
*   **Add Photos:** Upload your logo, screenshots of websites you've built, and "Team" photos (even if generated).
*   **Add Services:** Manually list "Web Design," "3D Websites," "E-commerce."
*   **Get Reviews:** **CRUCIAL.** Ask your first 5 clients (or friends) to leave a 5-star review mentioned "Web Design Monaghan".
