# Design System: Onyx & Code

## Status
**VERIFIED** (Agent A Approved)

## Color Palette
### Primary
- **Onyx**: `#0f0f0f` (Backgrounds, Deep Space)
- **Gold Accent**: `#D4AF37` (Highlights, Wireframes, Text Accents)

### Coffee Animation Palette (Specific)
- **Background**: `#9EBBCB` (Matte Light Blue/Grey)
- **Cup**: `#F5F5F1` (Off-white Matte Paper)
- **Sleeve**: `#966F50` (Cardboard Brown)
- **Lid**: `#1A1A1A` (Matte Charcoal/Black)
- **Liquid**: `#4B3621` (Glossy Dark Coffee)

## Typography
- **Font Family**: Inter, sans-serif
- **Headings**: Extrabold, tight tracking
- **Body**: Regular, readable, good contrast

## 3D Standards (Agent B)
- **Performance**: Must maintain 60fps.
- **Lighting**: Soft studio lighting with Environment presets.
- **Shadows**: Soft, contact shadows (Ambient Occlusion or blurred plane).
- **Interactivity**: OrbitControls enabled for product inspection.
